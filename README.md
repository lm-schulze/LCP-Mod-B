Exercises of the course *Laboratory of Computational Physics Mod. B*, A.Y 2023/24.

Exercises:
- **Exercise 1: Convolutional Neural Networks**. Training a CNN on the given sample data, fine-tuning parameters and exploring different optimizers (Adam, RMSprop, Nesterov)
- **Exercise 2: Data Visualization and Clustering**. Analysing and visualising high-dimensional data via Principal Component Analysis (PCA), comparing different clustering methods (DBSCAN, k-means, hierarchical clustering)
- **Exercise 3: XGBoost**. Training and tuning an XGBoost classifier, comparison to a simple feedforward NN
- **Exercise 4: Restricted Bolzmann Machines**. Studying the performance of an RBM and exploring its learned weights and biases
